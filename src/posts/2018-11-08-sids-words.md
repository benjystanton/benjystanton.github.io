<p>Our 2 year old has a unique language. I’ve made a note of the most common words he uses, so we can remember them after he stops.</p>

<dl>

  <dt>Hello</dt>
  <dd>Ay-oh</dd>

  <dt>Baby (as in himself)</dt>
  <dd>Bubba</dd>

  <dt>Milk</dt>
  <dd>Nooz</dd>

  <dt>Ice cream or yogurt</dt>
  <dd>A-nooz</dd>

  <dt>Car</dt>
  <dd>Boom-ba</dd>

  <dt>Sing</dt>
  <dd>Mah-moz</dd>

  <dt>Dance</dt>
  <dd>Nh-nh</dd>

  <dt>Sorry</dt>
  <dd>Yoy-oh</dd>

  <dt>Tummy</dt>
  <dd>Bdum-bdum</dd>

  <dt>Sweets</dt>
  <dd>Num-num</dd>

  <dt>Water</dt>
  <dd>Barf (as in bath)</dd>

  <dt>Jump</dt>
  <dd>Dump</dd>

  <dt>Bird</dt>
  <dd>Wack wack</dd>

  <dt>Dinosaur</dt>
  <dd>Raaas</dd>

  <dt>Dragon</dt>
  <dd>Wack wack a raaas</dd>

  <dt>Shark</dt>
  <dd>Do-do-do</dd>

</dl>
